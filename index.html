Brains Behind the Brand

Meet the minds who shape your journey here. More than just teachers, they are mentors committed to guiding you, challenging you and helping you realise your potential, every step of the way.

Commerce

Dr. Ananya Rao

Professor & Head, Department of Commerce

Dr. Rao brings over 18 years of academic and research experience in Finance and Corporate Strategy. She holds a Ph.D. in Commerce from Bangalore University and has completed her postdoctoral research in Behavioural Finance. A published author in leading international journals, her work focuses on financial decision-making and emerging market dynamics. Known for blending theory with real-world application, she mentors students toward careers in finance, research and entrepreneurship.

Technology

Dr. Vivek Menon

Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science

With a Ph.D. in Computer Science specialising in Artificial Intelligence, Dr. Menon has a strong background in machine learning and data systems. He has previously collaborated with industry leaders on applied AI solutions and has published multiple papers in reputed IEEE journals. His teaching approach emphasises problem-solving, innovation and hands-on learning, preparing students for careers in tech, research and digital transformation.

Psychology

Dr. Shreya Iyer

Professor, Department of Psychology

Dr. Iyer is a distinguished academic with a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology and over 15 years of teaching and consulting experience. Her expertise spans cognitive psychology, mental health and counselling practices. She has worked with hospitals and educational institutions to develop student well-being programmes. In the classroom, she is known for creating an engaging, discussion-driven environment that encourages critical thinking and empathy.

Biotechnology

Dr. Ayesha Khan

Professor, Department of Biotechnology

Dr. Khan holds a Ph.D. in Biotechnology with a specialisation in Molecular Genetics and brings over 16 years of experience in teaching and research. She has been a visiting scholar at leading international research institutions and has published extensively in high-impact journals in the fields of genomics and cellular biology. Her work focuses on translational research and its applications in healthcare and sustainability. In the classroom, she is known for her structured, concept-driven approach and for encouraging students to engage in research early in their academic journey.
